Manifesto

Purpose:


Creating new prespectives between generations by the aid of alternative toys, for the exploration of the family structure and invisible realities.


The main ideals we pursue with this platform are:

  • Bring new opportunities to the new generations.
  • Break with family roles and perfect relationships, such as gender, sex or sexuality.
  • Argument and comment on new ideas of self-knowledge and management.
  • Provide safe space for new generations, especially in the early stages of life.
  • Respect and fairness, among all species on the planet
